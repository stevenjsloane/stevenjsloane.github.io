Ritz

Two Bit Films was a fun video series that allowed us to show off the variety of Ritz Bits sandwich flavours through faux-creation stories. The name of the series is a play on 8-bit animation and represents the idea that all it takes is two crackers—or two seconds of video—to pack in a world of flavour.
